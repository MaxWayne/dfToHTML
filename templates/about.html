How does it work?

FAQ



What does SO stand for?

Significant other, the person you're breaking up with.

What if my SO doesn't show up?

We've started by targeting specific geographic areas. This to help increase the odds that they will show if the feeling is mutual. We will never directly notify your SO, unless they enter a matching record.

How much does CleanBreak cost?

It's free! It'd be great if you could spread the word and leave your story.

Can I just check if someone has entered my information?

No. You must be committed to ending the relationship to use the site. That's kind of the whole point.

 Can I take it back or delete my entry?

Yes. If you change your mind you can click here. If your SO enters a matching record before you delete yours, you will still both be notified.
